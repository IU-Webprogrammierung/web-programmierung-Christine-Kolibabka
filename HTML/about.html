About the artist

Born in 2018, the five-year-old prodigy has been captivating connoisseurs of contemporary art all over the globe ever since he first gripped a crayon in his chubby hands.

His whimsical explorations of color theory redefine the limits of possibility–and his depictions of war and peace strive to answer questions about the very essence of being human.

While the world holds its breath, awaiting yet another testament of his budding genius, he calmly focuses on improving his technique.

In his spare time, he likes to build Lego spacecraft and to think about the advent and fall of the Roman Empire.
